Definitions

There are various definitions that you should know related to drugs.

Adverse Effect

This is any undesired harmful effect resulting from any sort of medication, treatments or surgeries.

Adverse Drug Event

In patient safety literature the term Adverse Drug Event denotes a causal association between the drug and event. Adverse drug event includes adverse drug reactions and Medication errors. Adverse Drug Events can happen in places such as hospitals, long-term care settings, and outpatient settings.

Adverse Drug Reaction

It is an injury caused by some medication. It may occur due to:

  • Harm by drug use - is a response to a drug which is noxious and unintended
  • A medication error with or without harm
  • Wrong dose such as overdose and under dose
  • Wrong drug
  • Wrong infusion rate
  • Missed dose
  • lV infiltration
  • Wrong patient
