Aims

The aim of this module is to develop nurses’ understanding of the key processes and activities that enhance compassionate care for patients, their families and healthcare professionals.

Intended learning outcomes

After reading this module and completing the time out activities you should be able to:

  • Articulate the meaning of compassion in the context of self, patients and their families, healthcare professionals and the organisation.
  • Identify key processes that support the development of compassion in practice.
  • Reflect on your and others’ role and responsibilities in providing compassionate care in the healthcare setting.
  • Consider strategies that help to develop a culture of compassion across the wider organisation.
