What is Pharmacovigilance?

Pharmacovigilance is the science and activities relating to:

  • Detection
  • Assessment
  • Understanding
  • Prevention of adverse effects or any other drug related problems

Pharmacovigilance is concerned only about two outcomes regarding a drug. They are:

  • Safety
  • Efficacy

Pharmacovigilance is one of the most essential functions within a life science company. In order to develop, manufacture and commercialise a drug a company must adhere to strict regulations.
