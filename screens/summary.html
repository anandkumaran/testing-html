Summary

At the end of this module, you know that:

  • Adverse Effect is the undesired harmful effect resulting from any sort of medication, treatments or surgeries.
  • Adverse Drug Event is an injury resulting from the use of a drug.
  • Adverse Drug Reaction is an injury caused by some medication.
  • Pharmacovigilance is the science and activities relating to detection, assessment, understanding and prevention of adverse effects or any other drug related problems.
